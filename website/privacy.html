Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 19, 2026

TradeLab ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our Android application and website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: If you log in via Firebase (Google, Email, or Phone), we receive your basic profile info (name, email, profile picture).
  • Usage Data: We collect information about your trading simulations, quiz progress, and app interactions to provide the "Learn-to-Earn" features.
  • Device Information: We may collect device IDs for advertising and analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected data to maintain your simulated portfolio, track educational progress, and process virtual capital rewards. We also use it to personalize ads via Google AdMob and manage subscriptions via Google Play Billing.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication and analytics.
  • Google AdMob: For displaying rewarded video ads.
  • Google Play Billing: For processing premium subscriptions.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Since TradeLab is a simulator, no real financial data (bank accounts or real stock holdings) is ever processed by our app.
