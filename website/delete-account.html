Account Deletion Request

At TradeLab, we value your privacy. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Deletion

To request the deletion of your account, please send an email to our support team:

ashwathai.dev@gmail.com

Required Information

Please include the following details in your email from the email address associated with your account:

  • Subject: Account Deletion Request - TradeLab
  • Your registered Email Address or Phone Number.
  • A brief confirmation that you want all your simulated portfolio data and account details removed.

Data Deletion Process

Once we receive your request, we will verify your identity and process the deletion within 7 business days. This process will permanently remove:

  • Your profile information (Name, Email, Phone).
  • Your simulated portfolio, holdings, and transaction history.
  • Your educational quiz progress and unlocked virtual capital.

Note: This action is irreversible.
